Steelers Fest at Latrobe Memorial Stadium

August 2, 2019

Steelers Fest at Latrobe will feature the Steelers Ford F150 Truck, Pepsi tent, the six Steeler Lombardi trophies, Steelers Nation Unite tent, Game Faces face painting, Quarterback Toss game, food, and other activities.

Come meet Steelers Legend Jon Kolb from 4-5PM and Arthur Moats from 5-6PM
