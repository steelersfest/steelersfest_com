Steelers Fest at Latrobe Memorial Stadium

presented by Robindale Energy

August 5, 2022 :: 11 am - 5 pm

Latrobe is proud to have the Pittsburgh Steelers return for training camp in 2022! Steelers Fest at Latrobe happens before the players take to the field for the annual night practice.

The event is free and open to the public. This year will feature the Steelers Experience, autograph signings, outdoor activities, vendors, music, and fun for the entire family! Steeler fans can grab a bite to eat and take advantage of these unique experiences before the event wraps up with the live night practice, and a fireworks display.
